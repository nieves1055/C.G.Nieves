School History

Manolo Fortich National High School (MFNHS) is located in the Province of Bukidnon, Philippines. The school's history began with its founding, marking the start of its educational journey. Over time, the Dalirig Annex of MFNHS was separated from the main school and established as an independent institution, now known as Dalirig National High School. A significant milestone in the school's development occurred in February 2024, when MFNHS entered into a partnership with Northern Bukidnon State College. This collaboration resulted in the signing of a Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) for a Senior High School Work Immersion Program. The program is designed to provide students with opportunities to enhance their skills, work ethics, and explore potential career paths.
