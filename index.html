More Information

MFNHS was founded in 1963, with the vision of providing a nurturing and challenging educational environment. From its humble beginnings with a small number of students and teachers, it has grown into a reputable institution known for academic excellence and holistic development.

Throughout the years, we have achieved numerous milestones. We have consistently produced top-performing students in national examinations and have been recognized for our excellence in various fields, including sports, arts, and sciences. Our alumni have gone on to excel in various professions, contributing positively to society and embodying the values instilled in them during their time here.

The success of our school is not just measured by academic achievements but also by the strong sense of community and culture that we have cultivated. Our school values inclusivity, respect, and integrity, and these principles are evident in our daily interactions. Students, teachers, and staff work together to create a supportive and dynamic learning environment.

In the past 61 years, MFNHS has continually adapted to changes in the educational landscape. We have embraced new technologies, updated our curriculum, and introduced innovative teaching methods to ensure that our students are well-prepared for the future. Our commitment to continuous improvement is unwavering.
